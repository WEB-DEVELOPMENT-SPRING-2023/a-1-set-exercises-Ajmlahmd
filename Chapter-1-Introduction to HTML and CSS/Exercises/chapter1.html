Spaghetti Bolognese

This classic Italian dish is perfect for a family dinner.

Ingredients

  • 500g spaghetti
  • 500g beef mince
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 400g can chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato puree
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 beef stock cube
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Method

  1. Cook spaghetti according to packet instructions.
  2. In a large frying pan, cook beef mince over medium heat until browned.
  3. Add onion and garlic and cook until softened.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes, tomato puree, oregano, and crumbled stock cube.
  5. Simmer for 10 minutes or until sauce has thickened.
  6. Serve sauce over cooked spaghetti.
